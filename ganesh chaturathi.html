Ganesh Chaturthi: Celebrating the Remover of Obstacles

Ganesh Chaturthi is a Hindu festival honoring Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles. It is celebrated with great enthusiasm and fervor in India and around the world.

Significance of Ganesh Chaturthi

Ganesh Chaturthi marks the birth of Lord Ganesha, who is revered as the god of wisdom, prosperity, and good fortune. The festival is a time for spiritual growth, self-reflection, and celebration with family and friends.

Traditions and Celebrations

  • Installation of Ganesha idols in homes and public pandals
  • Offerings of modaks, flowers, and other sweets to Lord Ganesha
  • Recitation of prayers and hymns in praise of Lord Ganesha
  • Visarjan (immersion) of Ganesha idols in rivers or oceans

Ganesh Chaturthi Wishes

"Ganpati Bappa Morya" - a traditional greeting used during the festival
