Guru Purnima: Celebrating the Light of Knowledge

Guru Purnima is a traditional Hindu festival dedicated to spiritual and academic teachers, known as "gurus." It is celebrated on the full moon day in the Hindu month of Ashadha (June-July).

Significance of Guru Purnima

Guru Purnima is a day to honor and express gratitude to gurus who impart knowledge and wisdom. It marks the beginning of spiritual lessons and intensification of spiritual practices.

Celebrations

"Many followers visit ashrams and temples to participate in special programs and rituals. Some people set up altars at home with pictures or idols of their gurus and perform rituals.

Guru Purnima Quotes

"Gururbrahmaa gururvishnuh gururdevo Maheswarah | Guruh-saakshaat parambrahma tasmai shrigurave namah ||"

Importance of Guru Purnima

  • Honoring gurus who guide us towards knowledge and wisdom
  • Renewing vows and reflecting on teachings
  • Participating in special prayers and offerings
